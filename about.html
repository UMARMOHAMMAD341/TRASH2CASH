About the Project

TrashToCash is a clean, minimal marketplace that connects waste providers with recyclers. Sellers list recyclable materials (type, weight, price, location). Buyers discover and purchase materials to reuse or recycle. This system encourages sustainable behavior and provides income opportunities for local collectors.
